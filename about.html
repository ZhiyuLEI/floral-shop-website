About

Floral Flower Shop is owned and operated by Lux, Michael and Zavier. All members are highly trained professionals. With 10 years of experience in the floral industry, the team at Floral are able to create any floral item customers need. No matter delicate wedding items, corporate arrangement items, or romantic bouquets, our members can create special and unique items to suit the occasion.
We hope you can enjoy our work displayed on the website. All items can be customized to your requirements in terms of price, colour, size.
